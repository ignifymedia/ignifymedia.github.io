Who am I?

I'm a web developer and digital marketer helping retailers sell more online.

I started my web development career in 2011, where I learned Ruby and Ruby on Rails in a month in an internal dev bootcamp. I fell in love with Ruby and how pleasing it was to use. My first project was a web app that generated traffic reports by extracting Twitter data. Waze wasn't a thing back then.

Since then, I've used my web development skills in multiple industries: eCommerce (Spree, Shopify), Health and Fitness, Real Estate, Self-storage, Senior Living, Australian Magazine, and TV Show.

Then I came into digital marketing to help my parents up a traditional retailer/grocery business selling fast-moving consumer goods (FMCG). Our initial efforts helped us drum up customer awareness, which was crucial in generating more word-of-mouth. This sustained the business during the 2020 pandemic. 2 years in, it has grown into 2 grocery stores.

I believe that the heart of every sustainable business is the value it gives out to the world. Tech just helps elevate the value that is already there.

As such, I've decided to offer my problem-solving skills as Ignify Media to move the future of the web and commerce forward.
